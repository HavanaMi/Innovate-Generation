Our Goal

Our aim is to create collaboration, offer professional development, and promote young talent across artistic disciplines. We offer free mentoring, creative workshops, and the chance for participants to sell their work through exhibitions and events. Whether it’s fashion, photography, painting, sculpture or more – our programs are here to support emerging creators in building skills, confidence, and new futures.

Nikola’s Role

Nikola is the Director and Community Wellbeing Coordinator at Innovate Generation. With her background in forensic psychology, healthcare volunteering, and working with youth and elders, she ensures every young person feels welcome, safe, and empowered. She oversees mentoring activities and supports a positive and respectful environment across all our programs.
