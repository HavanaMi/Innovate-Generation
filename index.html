Welcome to Innovate Generation

Innovate Generation is a Community Interest Company (CIC) based in the UK. We organize creative and cultural events including fashion shows, photography exhibitions, sculpture showcases, canvas art, watercolors, sketches, and more – with opportunities for the public to purchase youth-made products. Our mission is to promote talented young people aged 14–25 and give them a platform to showcase their creative abilities.
