Welcome to Innovate Generation

Innovate Generation is a Community Interest Company (CIC) based in the UK. We organize creative and cultural events including fashion shows, photography exhibitions, sculpture showcases, canvas art, watercolors, sketches, and more – with opportunities for the public to purchase youth-made products. Our mission is to promote talented young people aged 14–25 and give them a platform to showcase their creative abilities.

Talent Spotlight: Nikola

Each year, we hold a special award event to recognize the most exceptional talents from our programs. Nikola is one of our shining stars, known for her artistic skills and outstanding academic achievements. Her paintings and creative energy serve as an inspiring example for other youth in our community. We’re proud to share her artwork with the world.

Our Goal

Our aim is to create collaboration, offer professional development, and promote young talent across artistic disciplines. We offer free mentoring, creative workshops, and the chance for participants to sell their work through exhibitions and events. Whether it’s fashion, photography, painting, sculpture or more – our programs are here to support emerging creators in building skills, confidence, and new futures.
