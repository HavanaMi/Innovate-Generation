About Us

Innovate Generation is a Community Interest Company (CIC) based in London, UK. We organize creative and cultural events including fashion shows, photography exhibitions, sculpture showcases, canvas art, watercolors, sketches, and more – with opportunities for the public to purchase youth-made products. Our mission is to promote talented young people, especially those aged 14–25, by giving them a platform to showcase their creative abilities, gain visibility, and generate income from their work. Through these opportunities, we aim to empower emerging artists and support them on their journey toward creative and financial independence.We operate in London with very ambitious plans. Everyone has some talent and qualities, and we want to help them discover what is best in themselves and pursue the career path they have always dreamed of. We understand that not everyone manages to follow the path they once set, which is why we want to help such individuals and guide them toward success as much as possible.

Our Office
